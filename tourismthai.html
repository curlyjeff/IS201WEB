A Travel Guide to Krabi, Thailand

- Stunning white sandy beaches, turquoise waters, limestone cliffs, and rich cultural heritage -

Here is some information about tourism in Krabi:

  1. Popular Attractions
    • Railay Beach - Known for its stunning white sand and breathtaking views
    • Thung Teao Forest Natural Park - Home to the Crystal Lagoon, a naturally warm and clear pool, perfect for a relaxing swim
    • Krabi Night Market - A unique shopping experience and a chance to try local cuisine
    • Tiger Cave Temple - A Beautiful buddhist temple
  2. Food Recommendations
    • Gecko Cabane Restaurant - A small gastronomique restaurant known for its unique dishes
    • Ruean-Mai Seafood Restaurant - Famous for its seafood dishes
    • 89 Cafe' - Offers a variety of dishes including vegetarian options
    • Kotung Restaurant - Known for its authentic Thai cuisine
  3. Hotel Recommendations
    • Islanda Hideaway Resort - A 4-star resort with a rating of 8 out of 10
    • Holiday Ao Nang Beach Resort, Krabi - A 3-star resort with a rating of 8 out of 10
  4. The best time to visit is between November and April to avoid unpredictable weather
